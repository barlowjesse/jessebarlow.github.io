Hi, my name is Jesse. I’m a freelance graphic designer who also likes to experiment with photography. I’m currently based in Holland, Michigan.

My design journey began in middle school, when my older brother introduced me to digital art and adobe software. Since then, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects, even including developing social media graphics for my high school’s sports teams. During my junior year of high school, I attended Careerline Tech Center for Graphic Design, I’ve utilized my skills and earned several certifications, including:

• Visual Design using Adobe Photoshop 2024
• Digital Video using Adobe Premiere Pro 2023
• Graphic Design and Illustration using Adobe Illustrator 2023
• Print and Digital Media Publication using Adobe InDesign 2024

If you're interested in working together or have any questions — feel free to get in touch via email.
